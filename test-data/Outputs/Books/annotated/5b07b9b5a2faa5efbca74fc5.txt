A summary report is given to the vice-president for accounts
payable. The report lists every provider who was paid that week, the
number of consultations each had, and his or her total fee for that
week. Finally, the total number of providers who provided services,
the total number of consultations, and the overall fee total are
printed.
Durinn the day, thh product is run in interactive mode to
allow operators to add new members to ChocAn, to delete members
who have resigned or suspend those whose dues are in arrears, and
to update member records. Similarly, provider records are added,
updated, and deleted.
